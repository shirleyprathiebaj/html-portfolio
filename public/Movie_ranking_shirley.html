Best Movies of All Time

My top 3 favourite movies

After The Promise (1987)

An american melodrama that shows the struggle of a father to keep his family united after his wife passes away.

Pursuit of Happiness (2006)

This movie is about a homeless father who fights against poverty and adversity and inspires us to follow our dreams.

The Karate Kid (2010)

This martial arts movie teaches us how self-control helps us to attain success.
